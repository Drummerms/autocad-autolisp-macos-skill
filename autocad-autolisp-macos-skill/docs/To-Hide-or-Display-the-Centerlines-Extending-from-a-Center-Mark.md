# To Hide or Display the Centerlines Extending from a Center Mark

Hide the centerlines extending of a center mark and redisplay them when needed.

## Hide or Display Centerlines for New Center Marks

1. At the Command prompt, enter CENTERMARKEXE.
2. Do one of the following:
   * Enter
     *0* (OFF) to add only a center mark.
   * Enter
     *1* (ON) to show centerlines that extend from the center mark.

## Hide or Display Centerlines for Existing Center Marks

1. Select one or more center marks.
2. If the Properties Inspector is not displayed, right-click and select Properties.
3. In the Properties Inspector, check or clear Show Extension.
