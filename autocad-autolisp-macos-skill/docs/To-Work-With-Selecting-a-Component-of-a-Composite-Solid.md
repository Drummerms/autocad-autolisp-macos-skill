# To Work With Selecting a Component of a Composite Solid

## Select a Face, Edge, or Vertex on the History Form of a Composite Solid

1. At the Command prompt, enter SUBOBJSELECTIONMODE and set the system variable to:
   * *1* for vertex selection
   * *2* for edge selection
   * *3* for face selection
2. On the history form, click a face, edge, or vertex.

   NOTE: If the Show History property for the history subobject is not turned on, you can display the history by pressing Ctrl as
   you move the cursor over the object.

## Select an Individual Solid That is Part of a Composite Solid

* Press Ctrl + click an individual solid that is part of a composite solid.
