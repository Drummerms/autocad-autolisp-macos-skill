# To Reorient the Model to the Home View

Do one of the following:

* Click the Home button located near the ViewCube. ![](../images/GUID-BD9C9619-86BF-4D7F-A398-20CF7F151C00-low.png)
* Right-click the ViewCube and choose Home.
