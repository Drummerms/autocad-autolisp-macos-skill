# To Remove Leaders From an Annotation

1. Select the multileader.
2. Do one of the following:
   * Hover over a leader endpoint grip, and choose Remove Leader from the grip menu.
   * On the Multileader toolbar, click Remove Leader, and then select the leaders you want to remove. Press ENTER.
