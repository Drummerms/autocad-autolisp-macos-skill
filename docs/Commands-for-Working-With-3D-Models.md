# Commands for Working With 3D Models

These commands and system variables are the basic commands for working with 3D models.

#### Commands

* [3DMOVE](GUID-35FD374D-CD9F-4EC6-B50E-7C368B67DFB5.htm "In a 3D view, displays the 3D Move gizmo to aid in moving 3D objects a specified distance in a specified direction.
  		")
* [3DORBIT](GUID-85CE824C-0AF4-4890-8487-ADBC92BF08F1.htm "Rotates the view in 3D space, but constrained to horizontal and vertical orbit only.
  		")
* [3DROTATE](GUID-A16B7027-1346-480F-AFDC-3A3A89EB08D8.htm "In a 3D view, displays the 3D Rotate gizmo to aid in revolving 3D objects around a base point.
  		")
* [3DSCALE](GUID-DCF3573A-268A-42D3-92F7-7B8A290C8427.htm "In a 3D view, displays the 3D Scale gizmo to aid in resizing 3D objects.
  		")
* [BOX](GUID-8B9B2875-3CA1-448D-ACF2-94C503DA54C6.htm "Creates a 3D solid box.
  		")
* [CONE](GUID-EF480870-AE25-4D6E-9E54-4708D711C127.htm "Creates a 3D solid cone.
  		")
* [CONVTOMESH](GUID-810D73BD-1C40-4985-AF31-60D9857F6E64.htm "Converts 3D objects such as polygon meshes, surfaces, and solids to mesh objects.
  		")
* [CONVTONURBS](GUID-0900AE84-39CD-4DC0-8B14-913BC350178B.htm "Converts 3D solids and surfaces into NURBS surfaces.
  		")
* [CONVTOSOLID](GUID-7A9C2DEA-961C-413D-BC20-D00654457599.htm "Converts 3D meshes and polylines and circles with thickness to 3D solids.
  		")
* [CONVTOSURFACE](GUID-7AF911DF-986F-4897-8651-921BE8710B14.htm "Converts objects to 3D surfaces.
  		")
* [CYLINDER](GUID-81157F00-D5A5-4307-AD0C-4332066DE2AE.htm "Creates a 3D solid cylinder.
  		")
* [EXTRUDE](GUID-85FD1106-8F10-4EE8-B0FB-99F1E3AEE405.htm "Creates a 3D solid or surface by extending a 2D or 3D curve.
  		")
* [LOFT](GUID-0A041818-2E32-4212-A3D8-CE0361C3D229.htm "Creates a 3D solid or surface in the space between several cross sections.
  		")
* [MESH](GUID-C0251558-62FC-4169-ADA2-DED728278556.htm "Creates a 3D mesh primitive object such as a box, cone, cylinder, pyramid, sphere, wedge, or torus.
  		")
* [PLANESURF](GUID-5E36FD08-A8AC-4ADC-B5BF-6162AE64E3BD.htm "Creates a planar surface.
  		")
* [POLYSOLID](GUID-22AE6A38-3404-4BF4-88C2-42CE3822ACD8.htm "Creates 3D wall-like solid.
  		")
* [PROJECTGEOMETRY](GUID-F884BC67-070F-4BAD-87EF-A8A77E23FB93.htm "Projects points, lines, or curves onto a 3D solid or surface from different directions.
  		")
* [PROPERTIES](GUID-DC3674C5-A4C7-4CF6-9148-9B124DF29B78.htm "Controls properties of existing objects.
  		")
* [PYRAMID](GUID-DA8A78E3-5954-415A-B279-E1C69F465D87.htm "Creates a 3D solid pyramid.
  		")
* [REVOLVE](GUID-6FF936FD-99BA-432A-A43B-4573FD924AF3.htm "Creates a 3D solid or surface by sweeping an object around an axis.
  		")
* [ROTATE](GUID-A28E361A-9A15-4DC6-BDDB-F543982AF6A8.htm "Rotates objects around a base point.
  		")
* [SPHERE](GUID-D003D7B2-A309-4C19-9CCF-AB9A7BA8833D.htm "Creates a 3D solid sphere.
  		")
* [SURFBLEND](GUID-BD4E2F9B-5B2D-462E-AE9B-E0A3B0324E6A.htm "Creates a continuous blend surface between two existing surfaces.
  		")
* [SURFNETWORK](GUID-FB04C267-5F15-4A63-B33A-ACA65356943E.htm "Creates a surface in the space between several curves in the U and V directions, including surface and solid edge subobjects.
  		")
* [SURFOFFSET](GUID-F242D0E4-0D58-4469-AE69-99BC842D0B25.htm "Creates a parallel surface a specified distance from the original surface.
  		")
* [SURFPATCH](GUID-F594A1AE-71B4-44D4-A867-AC95C43AF1E6.htm "Creates a new surface by fitting a cap over a surface edge that forms a closed loop.
  		")
* [SWEEP](GUID-2391CE97-3794-402C-8BC1-E2DCB452DD13.htm "Creates a 3D solid or surface by sweeping a 2D or 3D object or subobject along a path.
  		")
* [TORUS](GUID-F7F2E0D8-58D2-4068-BBC1-60740D1A024A.htm "Creates a donut-shaped 3D solid.
  		")
* [UCS](GUID-0BE49DA1-B323-4758-B49B-4C497D194C7A.htm "Sets the origin and orientation of the current user coordinate system (UCS).
  		")
* [VPORTS](GUID-BF45C5E3-ACA3-436A-B8E9-A17001A6F1D8.htm "Creates multiple viewports in model space or in a layout (paper space).
  		")
* [WEDGE](GUID-479D1F58-6629-430B-8E8A-901D646A55E6.htm "Creates a 3D solid wedge.
  		")

#### System Variables

* [DELOBJ](GUID-CB64587F-611E-441E-AB07-14B415BF535F.htm "Controls whether geometry used to create other objects is retained or deleted.
  		")
* [ORBITAUTOTARGET](GUID-4E7E1156-CADF-410C-BDD0-C209FDD8953E.htm " Controls how the target point is acquired for the 3DORBIT command.
  		")
* [SUBOBJSELECTIONMODE](GUID-7C4F6525-41DD-48A2-AE7C-45663EDD6122.htm "Filters whether faces, edges, vertices or solid history subobjects are highlighted when you roll over them.
  		")
