# To Connect Surfaces With a Fillet

1. Click
   Modeling tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface Fillet.
   ![](../images/GUID-FE809F4D-7C40-4881-B32C-AA446FAEE053-low.png)
2. Select the first and second surfaces.

   The fillet surface is created and the originating are surfaces trimmed if necessary.
