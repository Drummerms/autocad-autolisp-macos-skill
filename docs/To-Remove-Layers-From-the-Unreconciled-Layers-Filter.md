# To Remove Layers From the Unreconciled Layers Filter

After you confirm that a new layer conforms to your layer property requirements, you can remove it from the list of unreconciled
layers.

1. Click
   Window menu ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Layers.
2. In the Layer Properties Manager, Filters area, click the Unreconciled New Layers node in the Filters panel.
3. In the layer list, right-click a layer, and click Reconcile Layer.
