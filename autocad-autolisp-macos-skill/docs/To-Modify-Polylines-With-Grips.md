# To Modify Polylines With Grips

Polyline grips offer some grip-specific options that depend on the grip location (vertex or midpoint), the segment type (line
or arc), and the type of polyline (standard, curve-fit, or spline-fit.)

1. Select the polyline to display its grips.
2. Edit using one of the following methods:
   * *Stretch the polyline or its vertex.* Select a grip and drag it to the new location.
   * *Add a vertex.*Hover over a vertex ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Add Vertex. Specify a location for the new vertex.
   * *Extend a vertex.*Hover over an end vertex ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Extend Vertex. Specify a location for the new vertex. The vertex is added to your polyline extending from the selected end
     grip.
   * *Remove a vertex.*Hover over a vertex ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Remove Vertex.
   * *Convert a straight segment to an arc.* Hover over the midpoint grip on a straight segment ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Convert to Arc. Specify a location for the arc midpoint.
   * *Convert an arc to a line.*Hover over the midpoint of an arc segment ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Convert to Line.
   * *Convert to spline.*Right-click a polyline with straight line segments ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Polyline ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Curve Fit or Spline Fit.
   * *Edit a curve-fit polyline.*Select the polyline and drag the grips to a new location.
   * *Remove the curves from a spline.* Right-click the spline fit polyline ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Polyline ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Decurve.
