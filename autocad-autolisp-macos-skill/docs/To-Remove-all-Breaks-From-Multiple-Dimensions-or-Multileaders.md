# To Remove all Breaks From Multiple Dimensions or Multileaders

1. Click
   Drafting tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Dimension panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Dimension, Dimbreak.
   ![](../images/GUID-5FB2D06D-8AC6-48EA-8B7A-A1CF1FEDD526-low.png)
2. Enter
   *m* (Multiple), and press Enter.
3. Select the dimensions or multileaders from which to remove the dimension breaks, and press Enter.
4. Enter
   *r* (Remove), and press Enter.
