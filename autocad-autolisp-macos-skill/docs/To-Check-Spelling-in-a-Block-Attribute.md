# To Check Spelling in a Block Attribute

1. Click Tools menu![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif)Spelling . At the Command prompt, enter SPELL In the Check Spelling dialog box, click Settings.
2. In the Check Spelling Settings dialog box, click Block Attributes. Click OK.
3. In the Check Spelling dialog box, click Start.
4. Do *one* of the following:
   * To correct a word, select an alternate word from the Suggestions list or type a word in the Suggestions box. Click Change
     or Change All.
   * To leave a word unchanged, click Ignore or Ignore All.
   * To leave a word unchanged and add it to the dictionary, click Add to Dictionary.
5. Repeat step 5 for each misspelled word. Click Close to exit.
