# About Extending Surfaces

Create a new surface by extending it to meet the edge of another object or by specifying an extension length.

There are two types of extended surfaces: merge and append. The *merge* surface is a continuation of the surface with no seam. The *append* surface extends the surface by adding a second surface with a seam. Because it creates a seam, append surfaces have continuity
and bulge magnitude properties.

For both extended surface types, you can use the Properties Inspector to change the length.
