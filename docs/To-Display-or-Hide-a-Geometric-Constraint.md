# To Display or Hide a Geometric Constraint

1. Click
   Drafting tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Parametric panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Show Constraints.
   ![](../images/GUID-A302A903-6309-4AA1-B5E4-EA2713DAF5FC-low.png)
2. Select the constrained objects, and press Enter.
3. Choose one of following options:
   * *Show.* Displays the geometric constraints of the selected objects.
   * *Hide.* Hides the geometric constraints of the selected objects.
   * *Reset.* Displays the geometric constraints of the selected objects, and returns each contraint bar to its default location.
