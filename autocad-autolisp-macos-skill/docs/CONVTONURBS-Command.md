# CONVTONURBS (Command)

Converts 3D solids and surfaces into NURBS surfaces.

## Access Methods

*Tool Set*:
Modeling tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Convert to NURBS.
![](../images/GUID-121C46FF-0552-48F0-8518-537B00D66907-low.png)

*Menu*:
Modify ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface Editing ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Convert to NURBS.

## Summary

Converts solids and procedural surfaces to NURBS surfaces.

![](../images/GUID-45788DD1-8BF2-4213-9827-2FC44D5F4308-low.gif)

To convert meshes to NURBS surfaces, convert them to a solid or surface first with
[CONVTOSOLID](CONVTOSOLID-Command.md) or
[CONVTOSURFACE](CONVTOSURFACE-Command.md) and then convert them to NURBS surfaces.
