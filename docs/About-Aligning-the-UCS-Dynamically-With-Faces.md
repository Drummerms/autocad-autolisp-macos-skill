# About Aligning the UCS Dynamically With Faces

With the dynamic UCS turned on, you can temporarily and automatically align the *XY* plane of the UCS with a planar face on a 3D solid, a planar mesh element, or a planar point cloud segment while creating
an object.

During a command, you can align the UCS dynamically by moving your cursor over a planar face of a 3D solid or point cloud
rather than using the UCS command. After you finish the command, the UCS returns to its previous location and orientation.

You align the UCS dynamically by moving your cursor over a planar face or point cloud segment rather than using the UCS command.
After you finish the command, the UCS returns to its previous location and orientation.

NOTE:Dynamic UCS alignment does not detect planar surface objects or 2D geometry.
