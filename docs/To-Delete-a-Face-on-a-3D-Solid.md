# To Delete a Face on a 3D Solid

1. Click
   Modeling tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Solid panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Solid Edit - Face drop-down ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Delete Faces.
   ![](../images/GUID-5BE423D7-8E74-43DA-8C15-F09B96B64457-low.png)
2. Select the face to delete. (It must be surrounded by faces that share the same plane.)
3. Select additional faces or press Enter to delete.
4. Press Enter to complete the command.
