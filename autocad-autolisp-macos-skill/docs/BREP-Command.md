# BREP (Command)

Removes the history from 3D solids and composite solids, and associativity from surfaces.

## Summary

When a solid loses the history of the original parts from which it was created, the original parts can no longer be selected
and modified. BREP also removes surface associativity. When a surface loses associativity it loses any mathematical expressions
or information about how the surface was created.
