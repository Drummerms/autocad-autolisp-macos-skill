# SURFEXTEND (Command)

Lengthens a surface by a specified distance.

## Access Methods

*Tool Sets*:
Modeling tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface Extend.
![](../images/GUID-BD77B3F3-062E-4678-9296-4BBB2FEDFDA1-low.png)

*Menu*:
Draw ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) 3D Modeling ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surfaces ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Extend.

## Summary

The extension surface can be merged (part of the original surface) or appended (creating a second surface adjacent to the
original surface).

![](../images/GUID-FE7F76CF-F1D9-4E89-9F7C-2AEAA92792EF-low.gif)

## List of Prompts

The following prompts are displayed.

Specify Extension Distance
:   Specifies the extension length.

Expression
:   Enter a formula or equation to specify the length of the surface extension.

Modes
:   * *Extend* - Extrudes the surface in a way that tries to mimic and continue the shape of the surface.
    * *Stretch* - Extrudes the surface without trying to mimic and continue the shape of the surface.

Creation Type
:   * *Merge* - Extends the surface the specified distance without creating a new surface.
    * *Append* - Creates a new extension surface that is adjacent to the original surface. If the originating surface is a NURBS surface,
      the new extension surface will also be a NURBS surface.
