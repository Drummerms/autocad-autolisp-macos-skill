# To Convert a Solid Into a NURBS Surface

1. Click
   Modeling tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Convert to NURBS.
   ![](../images/GUID-121C46FF-0552-48F0-8518-537B00D66907-low.png)
2. Select a 3D solid object and press Enter.

The object is converted into multiple NURBS surfaces (each face becomes a separate surface.)

NOTE:To display the control vertices, click
Modify tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface CV - Show.
![](../images/GUID-8983AD29-5CBC-4507-A16D-95A497CABA9E-low.png)
