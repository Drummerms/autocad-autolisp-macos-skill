# Use Geometric Constraints

#### Topics in this section

* [About Geometric Constraints](About-Geometric-Constraints.md)
* [About Applying and Deleting Geometric Constraints](About-Applying-and-Deleting-Geometric-Constraints.md)

  You can apply geometric constraints to associate 2D geometric objects together, or specify a fixed location or angle.
* [About Displaying and Verifying Geometric Constraints](About-Displaying-and-Verifying-Geometric-Constraints.md)

  You can visually confirm what objects are associated with any geometric constraint, or what constraints are associated with
  any object.
* [About Modifying Geometrically Constrained Objects](About-Modifying-Geometrically-Constrained-Objects.md)

  You can edit geometrically constrained objects using grips, editing commands, or by relaxing or applying geometric constraints.
* [About Inferring Geometric Constraints](About-Inferring-Geometric-Constraints.md)

  You can automatically apply geometric constraints while creating and editing geometric objects.
