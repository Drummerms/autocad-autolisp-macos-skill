# To Create an Associative Center Mark

Creates an associative center mark on a circle or an arc.

1. Click
   Drafting tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Draw panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Centerline drop-down ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Center Mark.
   ![](../images/GUID-B272B416-A3D9-4ABD-A4FB-92E2A91E8D94-low.png)
2. Select a circle or an arc.
3. Continue selecting circles or arcs to place center marks.
4. Press Enter to end the command.

NOTE:Use DIMCENTER to create a non-associative center mark.
