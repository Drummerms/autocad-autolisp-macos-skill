# About Avoiding Abbreviations (DCL)

Abbreviations can be ambiguous and difficult to translate. If space constraints require you to abbreviate terms, abbreviate
them consistently within a group (such as a boxed column). Be consistent. Do not spell some terms in full and abbreviate others.
