# To Extend a Surface

1. Click
   Modeling tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface Extend.
   ![](../images/GUID-BD77B3F3-062E-4678-9296-4BBB2FEDFDA1-low.png)
2. Select one or more surface edges and press Enter.
3. Enter
   *m* (Modes) and specify the general shape of the extension:
   * To mimic and continue the shape of the surface based on the vector of adjacent edges: Enter
     *e*(Extend).
   * To extend the surface independent of the adjacent edges: Enter
     *s*(Stretch).
4. Specify whether the surface is merged or appended:
   * To merge the new surface to the original surface: Enter
     *m* (Merge).
   * To append the surface and create a separate surface: Enter
     *a* (Append).
5. Enter the distance of the extension and press Enter.
