# To Snap to a Geometric Point on an Underlay

1. Move your cursor over the desired object snap location.

   If UOSNAP is on, your cursor automatically locks onto the snap location in the drawing.
2. Select an object in the drawing.

   The cursor snaps to the eligible location closest to your selection.
