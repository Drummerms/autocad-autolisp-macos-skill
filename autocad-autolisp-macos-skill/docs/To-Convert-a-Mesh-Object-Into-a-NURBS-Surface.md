# To Convert a Mesh Object Into a NURBS Surface

1. Click
   Modeling tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Convert to Surface.
   ![](../images/GUID-4EC93940-F904-40DA-9989-C51F3CD39C26-low.png)
2. Select a mesh object and press Enter.

   The object is converted to a procedural surface.
3. Click
   Modeling tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Convert to NURBS.
   ![](../images/GUID-121C46FF-0552-48F0-8518-537B00D66907-low.png)
4. Click the surface object to convert it to a NURBS surface.

NOTE:To display the control vertices, click
Modeling tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface CV drop-down ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface CV - Show.
![](../images/GUID-8983AD29-5CBC-4507-A16D-95A497CABA9E-low.png)
