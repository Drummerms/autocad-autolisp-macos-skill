# To turn on or off the display of solid fills

When you turn off Fill mode, wide polylines, solid-filled polygons, gradient fill, and hatches are displayed in outline form.

1. Set Fill Mode to 0 to turn off the fill mode. This turns off the display of solid fills.
2. Set Fill Mode to 1 to turn on the fill mode. This turns on the display of solid fills.

   NOTE:Except for patterned hatches and gradient fills, solid fill is automatically turned off for hidden view and nonplan views
   in three dimensions.
3. At the Command prompt, enter *regen* to regenerate the drawing, updating the display.
