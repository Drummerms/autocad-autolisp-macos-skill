# To Turn On and Turn Off Polar Tracking

* Press F10, or click the Polar Tracking button on the status bar.
  ![](../images/GUID-519E604B-11D3-41C7-BDD7-35141FA899C4-low.png) Find

NOTE:To turn polar tracking on or off temporarily, hold down the F10 key while you work. The direct distance entry method is not
available while you are using the temporary override key for polar tracking.
