# To Move Text to the Left Side of the Dimension Line

1. Click
   Drafting tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Dimension panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Dimension Text Edit drop-down ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Left
   .
   ![](../images/GUID-C4B12926-9AE5-463B-BF66-656797F522E6-low.png)
2. Select the dimension.

   The dimension text is left-justified along the dimension line inside the extension lines.
