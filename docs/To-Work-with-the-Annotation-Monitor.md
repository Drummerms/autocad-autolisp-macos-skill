# To Work with the Annotation Monitor

Turns the annotation monitor on or off.

## To Turn on or off the Annotation Monitor

When the annotation monitor is on, it flags all non-associative annotations by placing a badge on them.

1. On the command line, enter ANNOMONITOR.
2. Enter *2* to turn on the Annotation Monitor
3. Enter *1* to turn on the Annotation Monitor
4. Enter *0* to turn off the Annotation Monitor
5. Enter *-1* to turn off the Annotation Monitor
6. Enter *-2* to turn on the Annotation Monitor.

   The annotation monitor automatically turns on (ANNOMONITOR=2) as soon as a model documentation edit or update event occurs.
