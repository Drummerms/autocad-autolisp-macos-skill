# To Change the Frequency of Major Grid Lines

1. Click
   Tools ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Drafting Settings menu.
2. In the Drafting Settings dialog box, Snap and Grid tab, under Grid Spacing, specify a number for Major Line Every.
