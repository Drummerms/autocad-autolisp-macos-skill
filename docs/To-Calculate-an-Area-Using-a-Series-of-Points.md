# To Calculate an Area Using a Series of Points

1. Click
   Tools ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Inquiry ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Area.
2. Specify a series points in a sequence that outlines the perimeter the area, and press Enter.

NOTE:You do not need to duplicate the first and last points.
