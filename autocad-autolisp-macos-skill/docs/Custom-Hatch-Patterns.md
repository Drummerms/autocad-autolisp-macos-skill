# Custom Hatch Patterns

#### Topics in this section

* [About Custom Hatch Patterns and Hatch Pattern Definitions](About-Custom-Hatch-Patterns-and-Hatch-Pattern-Definitions.md)

  A standard library of hatch patterns is provided with the product that can be used as they are or modified to suit your needs.
  You can also create your own custom hatch patterns.
* [About Hatch Patterns With Multiple Lines](About-Hatch-Patterns-With-Multiple-Lines.md)

  Complex hatch patterns can contain multiple definition lines and can have an origin that passes through offsets from the origin.
* [About Hatch Patterns with Dashed Lines](About-Hatch-Patterns-with-Dashed-Lines.md)

  Hatch patterns can contain dashed-lines by adding dash-lengths to a hatch pattern definition line.
* [FAQ: Why can't I use my custom hatch pattern (PAT) files?](FAQ-Why-can't-I-use-my-custom-hatch-pattern-PATfiles.md)
