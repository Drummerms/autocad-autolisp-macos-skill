# Activity Insights

## Search and Filter

We've simplified our search and filter bars for ease of use. Now, you can filter activities by time, users, and specific activities
with one button.

![](../images/GUID-BD90E728-77BE-4885-BAC3-A4D4A8791A2E-low.png)

You can also right-click any activity to quickly filter by the user who performed the activity or by the same type of activity.

![](../images/GUID-579D7BA5-B031-46BA-9E03-DFE02063CDD1-low.png)

Here are other enhancements made to activities:

* Renamed
  *Viewed* to
  *Opened* and turned it on by default.
* Added a new activity for the SHARE command.
* Made the drawing name in an Xref activity tile clickable for file-open actions.
