# About Modifying Surfaces

Fillet, extend, and trim surfaces or modify NURBS surfaces with control vertices.

Procedural and NURBS surfaces can be edited with basic editing tools such as trimming, extending and filleting. NURBS surfaces
can be modified with these tools as well, but they can also be reshaped by stretching control vertices. When your surface
design is complete, use the surface analysis tools to ensure the quality of your model and to rebuild it if necessary.
