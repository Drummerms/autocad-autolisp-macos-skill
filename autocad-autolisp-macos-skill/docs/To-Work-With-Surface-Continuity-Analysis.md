# To Work With Surface Continuity Analysis

## Analyze Surface Continuity

1. Click
   Modeling tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Analysis drop-down ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Analysis Zebra.
   ![](../images/GUID-DF956D9B-DA0A-4419-85BA-7C3CE54FDA07-low.png)
2. Select a surface and press Enter.

   The zebra stripes are displayed.

## Turn Off the Zebra Display

1. Click
   Modeling tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Analysis Options.
   ![](../images/GUID-E43E5959-FA91-43AA-B25D-2754135C2B20-low.png)
2. In the Zebra tab of the Analysis Options dialog box, click Clear Zebra Analysis.

   The zebra stripes disappear.

## Change the Zebra Analysis Options

1. Click
   Modeling tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Analysis Options.
   ![](../images/GUID-E43E5959-FA91-43AA-B25D-2754135C2B20-low.png)
2. In the Zebra tab of the Analysis Options dialog box, select the desired options.
