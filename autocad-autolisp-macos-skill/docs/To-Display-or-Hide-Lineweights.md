# To Display or Hide Lineweights

Click the Show/Hide Lineweight button
![](../images/GUID-1181E2DA-4C54-47A5-B32E-D79762F0CE7C-low.png)on the status bar.

NOTE:If no change is visible, it's probably due to a combination of the thickness of the line compared to the display resolution
of your monitor.
