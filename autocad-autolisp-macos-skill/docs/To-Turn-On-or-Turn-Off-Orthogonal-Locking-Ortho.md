# To Turn On or Turn Off Orthogonal Locking (Ortho)

* Press F8, or click the Ortho button on the status bar.
  ![](../images/GUID-51508211-363E-462E-A9B4-F4B36B08350B-low.png) Find

  The status bar is located in the lower right-hand corner of the application window.

NOTE:Turning on automatically turns off polar tracking.

To turn Ortho off temporarily, hold down the Shift key while you work. Direct distance entry is not available with this override.
