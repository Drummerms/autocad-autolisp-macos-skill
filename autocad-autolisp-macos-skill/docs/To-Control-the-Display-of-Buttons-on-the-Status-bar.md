# To Control the Display of Buttons on the Status bar

1. Click Settings at the far right end of the Status bar.
   ![](../images/GUID-3811A988-85ED-4568-A59C-A09C6074AEC6-low.png)
2. Select or clear a check box to change whether the button is displayed or hidden.
