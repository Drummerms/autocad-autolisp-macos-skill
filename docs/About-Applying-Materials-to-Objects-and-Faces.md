# About Applying Materials to Objects and Faces

You can apply materials to objects, layers, or faces.

You can apply materials to a face on an object, an entire object, or all objects on a layer.

![](../images/GUID-BAE062CA-0B5C-42E9-8FF5-6632C6057875-low.png)

Materials in the Materials Browser can be applied to a 3D object by

* Selecting objects in the drawing to assign the material and then clicking a material in the library.

  As an alternative to clicking a material in a library, you can right-click on a material in the library and choose Assign
  to Selection from the shortcut menu.
* Dragging a material from a library and dropping it onto an object in the drawing.

NOTE:When selecting objects, you can select individual faces by holding down the Ctrl key prior to selecting objects in the drawing.
