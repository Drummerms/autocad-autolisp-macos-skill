# To Convert a Drawing from Inches to Centimeters

Change the drawing scale to resize all objects.

1. Click
   Drafting tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Modify panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Scale.
   ![](../images/GUID-2E69AFE3-06D8-4B6B-AFAE-C8CB3FB009E8-low.png)
2. At the Select Objects prompt, enter
   *all*.
3. Enter a base point of
   *\*0,0*.

   Scaling will be relative to the world coordinate system (WCS) origin and the location of the drawing origin will remain at
   the WCS origin.
4. Enter a scale factor.

   For example, to convert from inches to centimeters enter 2.54 (there are 2.54 centimeters per inch). Or, to convert from
   centimeters to inches enter 0.3937 (the inverse of 2.54 centimeters per inch).
