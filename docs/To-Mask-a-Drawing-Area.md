# To Mask a Drawing Area

1. Click
   Draw ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Wipeout.
2. Specify points in a sequence that defines the perimeter of the area to be masked.

   You can also enter
   *p* and click an existing polyline to use. The polyline must be closed, contain line segments only, and have zero width.
3. Press Enter to end the command.
