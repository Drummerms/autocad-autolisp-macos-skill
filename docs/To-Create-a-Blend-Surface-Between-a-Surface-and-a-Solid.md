# To Create a Blend Surface Between a Surface and a Solid

1. Click
   Modeling tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface Creation drop-down ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Surface Blend.
   ![](../images/GUID-F583F639-0757-4578-B365-1EB760924257-low.png)
2. In the drawing area, select a surface edge and a solid edge.
3. Specify the continuity and bulge magnitude for each edge and press Enter.
