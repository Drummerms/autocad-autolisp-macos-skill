# To Work With Center Mark and Centerline Associativity

Redefine or remove the associativity between center marks or centerlines and the objects that they define.

## Reassociate a Center Mark or Centerline

TIP:Turn on the annotation monitor to track the associativity of the center marks or centerlines. Click the badge to display options
specific to the disassociated center mark or centerline.

1. At the Command prompt, enter CENTERREASSOCIATE.
2. Select a center mark or centerline.
3. Select any of the following:
   * A circle or an arc to reassociate a center mark
   * Two lines to reassociate a centerline

## Disassociate a Center Mark or Centerline

1. At the Command prompt, enter CENTERDISASSOCIATE.
2. Select one or more center marks or centerlines.
3. Press Enter.
