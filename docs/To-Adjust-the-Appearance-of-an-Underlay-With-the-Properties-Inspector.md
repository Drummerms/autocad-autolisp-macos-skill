# To Adjust the Appearance of an Underlay With the Properties Inspector

1. Double-click the underlay to display the Properties Inspector.
2. Do one of the following:
   * Adjust the contrast by entering a new value.
   * Adjust the fade by entering a new value.
   * Turn Monochrome On or Off.
   * Set Adjust Colors for Background to Yes or No.

The appearance of the underlay is updated in real-time as you make adjustments.
