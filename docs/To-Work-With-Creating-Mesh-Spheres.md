# To Work With Creating Mesh Spheres

## Create a Mesh Sphere

1. Click
   Modeling tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Mesh panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Primitive drop-down ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Sphere.
   ![](../images/GUID-D22E5B03-04EA-4FE6-BB76-3890875E68AE-low.png)
2. Specify the center of the sphere.
3. Specify the radius or diameter of the sphere.

## Create a Mesh Sphere Defined by Three Points

1. Click
   Modeling tab ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Mesh panel ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Primitive drop-down ![](../images/ac.menuaro.gif) Sphere.
   ![](../images/GUID-D22E5B03-04EA-4FE6-BB76-3890875E68AE-low.png)
2. Specify the center of the sphere.
3. Specify the radius or diameter of the sphere.
